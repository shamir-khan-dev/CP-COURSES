Neuroplasticity
New evidence suggests that our brains are not
fixed at an early age. In fact, the brain at all
ages can and is constantly re-wiring itself
based on our experiences, and even our
thoughts.
According to Nicholas Carr, we are not
hardwired, instead, the genius of our
brains construction is not that it contains
a lot of hardwiring, but that it doesnt
This is the idea of Neuroplasticity.
